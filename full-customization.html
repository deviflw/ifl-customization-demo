Frequently Asked Questions

Can I send you my own watch for customization? ‚ñº
Currently, we only work with new watches from our curated collection. This ensures warranty validity, quality control, and proper insurance coverage throughout the customization process.
What's included in the 2 free revisions? ‚ñº
You can request up to 2 rounds of major design changes to the digital mockup. Minor adjustments (small color tweaks, tiny position changes) don't count toward your revision limit. Additional major revisions beyond 2 are available for $100 each.
Can I see examples similar to my idea? ‚ñº
Absolutely! After you submit your Vision Board, we'll share relevant examples from our portfolio to help refine your concept. You can also browse our Inspiration Gallery above for general style references.
What's your warranty coverage? ‚ñº
Every customized watch comes with a 2-year IFL warranty covering the artwork and customization. The base watch retains its original manufacturer warranty. We also provide lifetime support for any questions about your unique timepiece.
Do you handle corporate orders? ‚ñº
Yes! We love creating custom watches for companies, teams, and special events. For orders of 5+ watches, we offer special pricing and dedicated project management. Contact us at corporate@iflwatches.com for details.
