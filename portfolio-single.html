The Story

The Ocean Explorer transforms the Oris Aquis Date into a canvas celebrating marine life. Each brushstroke captures the fluid movement of ocean currents, while vibrant blues and teals mirror the depths of the sea. This hand-painted masterpiece brings the underwater world to your wrist, featuring intricate details of coral reefs and marine creatures that seem to dance with every movement of your hand.
